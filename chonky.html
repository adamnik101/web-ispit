More info

Chonky is very sweet once she gets to know you but this will take some time. If her body language is understood and she is given her space to adjust to her new surroundings she will make a super companion.

Chonky can be very vocal and gets herself quite anxious around other dogs.

Quiet dog free areas for her to walk is a must. A quiet home environment would be great to allow her to settle into family life at her own pace.
