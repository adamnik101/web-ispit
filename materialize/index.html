About us

Dogfinder is an online, searchable database of animals who need homes. It is also a directory of nearly 8,000 animal shelters and adoption organizations across the United States. Organizations maintain their own home pages and available-dog databases.

Our Mission

To use Internet technology and the resources it can generate to:

  1. Increase public awareness of the availability of high-quality adoptable dogs
  2. Increase the overall effectiveness of dog adoption programs across United States
  3. Elevate the status of dogs to that of family member

From the comfort of their personal computers, dog lovers can search for a dog that best matches their needs.

Dogfinder is updated DAILY.
