About Dog Adoption

Ask anyone who has adopted a dog and they’ll share with you their story of love, fun and companionship. Why? Because shelter dogs are amazing!

Within the next year, millions of dogs will enter shelters and rescue groups nationwide. In that time, even more people intend to bring a dog into their homes, which is why we’re working to show why shelters should be your first choice and preferred way to acquire a dog.

Adopting a dog from a shelter or rescue is a win-win, for you and the dog. So, what are you waiting for? Begin your search for dogs available for adoption near you today.
